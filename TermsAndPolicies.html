EcoBot Admin Terms and Policies

The following terms and policies are intended for administrators of the EcoBot Arduino-based vending machine system, which facilitates the exchange of empty plastic bottles for food rewards. As an administrator, your role is critical in ensuring the system functions reliably, ethically, and securely.

By accessing the EcoBot admin panel, you agree to the following policies:

  • Authorized Access Only: Only verified administrators are permitted to use the admin panel. Account credentials must remain confidential and not be shared under any circumstances.
  • System Oversight: Admins are expected to monitor the vending machine’s operations, including bottle detection, reward dispensing.
  • Data Privacy: User data such as names, timestamps, and recycling activity must be handled securely and only used for internal reporting and maintenance.
  • Inventory Management: Admins must routinely inspect the food reward inventory to ensure items are available, safe for consumption, and within expiration dates.
  • Technical Maintenance: Regular checks of hardware components (e.g., load cell, servo motor, LCD display, proximity sensors) are required to prevent system failures.
  • User Support: Any reports of malfunction or failed transactions must be logged and resolved promptly to maintain trust and system usability.
  • System Updates: Any firmware, database, or configuration updates must be tested before implementation to avoid introducing errors to the live system.
  • Security Compliance: Admins are responsible for preventing unauthorized access and ensuring that the machine is securely enclosed and monitored.
  • Accountability: Misuse of administrative access, data, or system components may result in suspension of privileges and further disciplinary action.

Administrators play an essential role in upholding the mission of EcoBot — promoting environmental sustainability and rewarding responsible recycling behavior. These terms exist to support consistent operations and to safeguard the interests of both users and system stakeholders.

For questions or clarifications, contact the EcoBot support team.
